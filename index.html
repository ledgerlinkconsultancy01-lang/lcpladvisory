FAQ's

Common Questions Answered

What services does LedgerLink Consultation provide?

+

LedgerLink provides three core service pillars: Finance Core (accounting, bookkeeping, compliance, TDS, payroll, audits), Tax Strategy (founder tax planning, business structuring, ESOP advisory, capital gains planning, international taxation), and Tax Defense (GST litigation, Income Tax appeals, scrutiny handling, ITAT/CIT(A) representation). We are a one-stop financial advisory firm for businesses of all sizes.

How do I get started with LedgerLink?

+

Getting started is simple. You can reach us via the Contact page, call +91 82388 39433, or WhatsApp us directly. We schedule a free initial consultation to understand your needs, after which we propose a customized engagement plan with clear deliverables and transparent pricing.

Do you serve clients outside Ahmedabad?

+

Absolutely. While headquartered in Ahmedabad, we serve clients across Gujarat and pan-India. Most services — including compliance filings, tax planning, and virtual CFO — are delivered remotely. For litigation requiring physical presence, we coordinate with our pan-India network of advocates and chartered accountants.

I received a GST/Income Tax notice. What should I do?

+

Don't panic — but act immediately. Most notices have strict response timelines (typically 15–30 days). Contact us right away via WhatsApp or phone. We will review the notice, advise on the appropriate response strategy, draft a comprehensive reply, and represent you before the department if required. Early action significantly improves outcomes.

What is the cost of your services?

+

Our fees are competitive and transparent, structured based on complexity and scope. We offer monthly retainer plans for ongoing accounting, fixed-fee packages for specific filings, and milestone-based billing for litigation. We provide a detailed fee proposal before commencing any engagement — no hidden charges whatsoever.

How do you ensure confidentiality of my financial data?

+

Confidentiality is the cornerstone of our practice. All client data is stored in encrypted, access-controlled systems. We sign NDAs with every client upon engagement. Our staff undergoes regular data privacy training. We do not share client information with any third party without explicit consent, fully complying with applicable data protection laws.

Can LedgerLink help with new business registration and startup compliance?

+

Yes! We provide complete startup support including company incorporation (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC), GST registration, MSME registration, Startup India recognition, professional tax, and shop & establishment registration. We also advise on optimal entity selection and initial business structuring to ensure you start on the right financial footing.

What software and tools do you use for accounting?

+

We work with all major accounting platforms including Tally Prime, QuickBooks, Zoho Books, Busy, and SAP. We also use specialized tools for GST filing (ClearTax, GSTN portal), income tax (Taxmann, Winman), payroll (GreytHR, Keka), and MCA/ROC filings. We adapt to your existing systems or help you migrate to more efficient platforms.

How quickly can you respond to urgent tax matters?

+

Our standard response time for ongoing clients is within 4 business hours. For urgent matters like search and survey situations, imminent notice deadlines, or demand orders, we provide same-day response and emergency consultation. Reach us directly on WhatsApp at +91 82388 39433 for urgent matters — available 6 days a week.

Do you provide Virtual CFO services?

+

Yes. Our Virtual CFO service is ideal for startups and SMEs that need senior financial expertise without the cost of a full-time CFO. This includes financial planning & analysis, investor reporting, board presentations, fundraising support, budget management, and strategic financial advisory — delivered by experienced CAs at a fraction of the in-house cost.
